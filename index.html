Policy settings

Define the rules that run your control workflow

User-defined

This is configurable by the investor, not hard-coded by the product. You decide which conditions stay informational, which create a review, and which move into higher-priority attention.

Review cadence 30 days

Trigger review if a core position has not been revisited in 30 days.

Drift threshold Medium

Escalate when thesis evidence no longer matches the original case.

Single-name concentration 12%

Flag any position that crosses the sizing rule and push it into review.

Overlap threshold High

Escalate linked exposures when overlap creates a higher-priority review item.

Decision retention Always on

Keep adds, trims, holds, and exits attached to the position so future review has context.

Core workflow

Everything runs through the Review Queue.

The Review Queue is the operating surface of Portfolio OS. It tells you what needs review now, why it is there, what changed, and what action is required.

Not notifications. Not reminders. A working system for running the portfolio.

Example

How a review gets created

A position drifts from your thesis and also moves beyond your sizing rule. Portfolio OS creates a review item, raises its priority, brings back the original rationale, and helps you decide whether to hold, trim, or exit.
